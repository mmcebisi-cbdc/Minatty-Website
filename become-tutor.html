Application Process

Follow these 6 steps to join our tutor community

1

Online Application

Complete our online application form with your personal information, education background, and teaching experience. This takes about 10-15 minutes.

2

Upload Qualifications

Submit copies of your academic qualifications, teaching certificates (if applicable), and any relevant credentials. Accepted formats: PDF, JPG, PNG.

3

Identity Verification

Verify your identity by uploading a valid government-issued ID. This ensures safety and trust on our platform for all users.

4

Background Check

We conduct a thorough background check to ensure the safety of our students. This process typically takes 3-5 business days.

5

Interview & Screening

Participate in a brief online interview with our team. We'll discuss your teaching experience, methodology, and answer any questions you have about the platform.

6

Approval & Onboarding

Once approved, you'll receive onboarding materials, platform training, and can start creating your profile. Welcome to the Minatty Learning Hub family!

How Tutors Get Paid

Transparent, fair, and reliable payment structure

💲

Set Your Rate

Choose your hourly rate based on your qualifications and experience. Typical range: R100-R350/hour.

📊

Platform Fee

We charge a platform fee (25%) to cover payment processing, support, and platform maintenance.

🔍

Background Check Fee

A once off payment of R250 is required to cover the cost of thorough tutor background checks, ensuring a safe and trusted platform for all students.

⏱️

Weekly Payments

Get paid weekly via bank transfer. Fast, secure, and reliable payment processing every Friday.
