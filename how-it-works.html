How It Works

Getting started with Minatty Learning Hub is easy. Follow these simple steps to find your perfect tutor and start improving your results.

Step 1

🔍 Search for a Tutor

Use our powerful search and filter tools to find tutors that match your needs. Filter by subject (Math or Physical Science), grade level, location, hourly rate, and availability.

Search Features:

  • ✓ Filter by subject and grade level
  • ✓ Choose online or in-person
  • ✓ Set your budget with price filters
  • ✓ Search by location for in-person tutoring
Step 2

👤 View Tutor Profiles

Browse detailed tutor profiles to find the perfect match. Review qualifications, experience, teaching style, and honest student reviews to make an informed decision.

Profile Information:

  • ✓ Academic qualifications and certifications
  • ✓ Teaching experience and specializations
  • ✓ Student ratings and detailed reviews
  • ✓ Hourly rates and availability
Step 3

📅 Book a Lesson

Found the right tutor? Book your first lesson in just a few clicks. Choose between a trial lesson or commit to regular sessions. Select your preferred time and format (online or in-person).

Booking Options:

  • ✓ Book a trial lesson or full sessions
  • ✓ Choose online or in-person format
  • ✓ Flexible scheduling to fit your calendar
  • ✓ Secure payment processing
Step 4

🎓 Attend Sessions & See Results

Attend your tutoring sessions and watch your understanding grow. Track your progress, practice with homework support, and see your grades improve. Most students see measurable results within the first month!

What to Expect:

  • ✓ Personalized one-on-one attention
  • ✓ Customized lesson plans for your needs
  • ✓ Homework help and exam preparation
  • ✓ Regular progress tracking and feedback

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our tutoring process

How much do tutoring sessions cost?

Tutor rates vary based on experience and qualifications, typically ranging from R150-R400 per hour. You can filter tutors by your budget and see exact rates on each tutor's profile.

Can I try a tutor before committing?

Yes! Many tutors offer trial lessons at a discounted rate so you can ensure they're the right fit before booking regular sessions.

How are tutors verified?

All tutors undergo a rigorous screening process including qualification verification, background checks, identity verification, and interviews before being approved on our platform.

What if I'm not happy with my tutor?

You can switch tutors at any time. We want you to find the perfect match, and our support team is here to help you find another tutor if needed.

How long are tutoring sessions?

Most sessions are 1-2 hours, but the duration is flexible based on your agreement with the tutor. You can book single sessions or packages.

What happens if l miss a lesson?

If there was no prior communication, you will unfortunately not get a refund. However if communication is made at least 24 hours before the scheduled lesson, we could re-schedule your lesson or give you a refund depending on what student prefers.
