📐 Mathematics

Master mathematical concepts aligned with South African CAPS and IEB curriculum. Our qualified tutors break down complex topics into easy-to-understand lessons tailored to your learning style.

CAPS & IEB Curriculum Content:

  • ✓ Number & Number Systems: Real & non-real numbers, surds, exponents, logarithms
  • ✓ Algebra: Equations, inequalities, linear programming, sequences & series
  • ✓ Functions: Linear, quadratic, exponential, hyperbolic, trigonometric functions
  • ✓ Trigonometry: Compound & double angles, trig equations, sine & cosine rules
  • ✓ Analytical Geometry: Coordinate geometry, lines, circles, tangents
  • ✓ Calculus: Limits, differentiation, cubic functions, rate of change (Grade 12)
  • ✓ Statistics & Probability: Data handling, regression, normal distribution

🔬 Physical Sciences

Excel in both Physics and Chemistry with comprehensive tutoring aligned with CAPS and IEB curriculum. Understand complex scientific principles through practical examples and expert guidance.

CAPS & IEB Curriculum Content:

  • ✓ Physics - Mechanics: Newton's laws, work, energy, power, momentum
  • ✓ Physics - Waves: Transverse & longitudinal waves, sound, electromagnetic radiation
  • ✓ Physics - Electricity: Electrostatics, circuits, series & parallel, Ohm's law
  • ✓ Chemistry - Matter & Materials: Atomic structure, periodic table, chemical bonding
  • ✓ Chemistry - Reactions: Acids & bases, redox, chemical equilibrium, stoichiometry
  • ✓ Organic Chemistry: Hydrocarbons, functional groups, polymers
  • ✓ Practical Work: Laboratory techniques, safety, scientific investigations
